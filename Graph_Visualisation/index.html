How does stigma affect your life?

This is a collaborative project co-designed with the peer support group Red Ribbon Living Well. The aim is to visualize the impact of HIV stigma on people's lives. Each node represents a different aspect of stigma and links to key themes. Click on the smaller leaf nodes to display quotes from the group.
